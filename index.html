Judex

Judex is a simple application designed to improve your SMS experiences regardless of what messaging app you choose to use. By allowing Judex to take control of SMS notifications, you can filter which messages you are notified by, using factors such as the sender and the content of the message, and can even allow Judex to respond to messages for you.
